Be Strong For Mother

  • Originality: The original concept of the game is a single player platformer, but I added a 2nd player element to it, there is a cube roaming around the map following the cursor.
    The 2nd player has to click on the cursor to shake the screen, adding a layer of difficulty.
  • Technical Merit: Having a object follow the cursor.
  • Prototype Postmortem: The built in physics for Phaser is very finicky. A small decimal value change created an extremely large change.
  • Prototype Assets: I used all the assets from myfirstgame example and changed the background to a silly meme.
    • Background image: Be Strong For Mother
    • Bomb image: bomb
    • Dude image: dude
    • Platform image: platform
    • Star image: star
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I used the overall code from myfirstgame found here: myfirstgame. I also used some of the code from Digital Assignment 0, specifically the mouse tracking function.
  • High Concept: Collect as many stars possible!
  • Challenge: Two players, but locally only.
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard and moust input. Randomness from how the cube, stars, and bombs move.
  • Prototype Goal: A platformer with a collect-a-thon.
  • Player Experience Goals: Frustration.
  • Gameplay: Player 1 moves the character using the left and right keys, which allows the character to move left and right respectively.
    Player 2 moves using the mouse. They must left click on the cube, which then shakes the entire screen, making it difficult for player 1 to focus.
    Player 1 needs to collect as many stars before they get hit by a bomb.
  • Strategies: Player 1 needs to focus on finding the fastest way to the upper platforms, as they have more stars spawned towards the top.
    Player 2 needs to find a steady rhythm to be able to click on the cube quickly and frequently to confuse player 1.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: This is a very simple game with no overall meaning. Just a test on javascript and phaser.
  • Target Audience: A fun time killer for younger siblings.
  • Play Time: 1~5 minutes long.
