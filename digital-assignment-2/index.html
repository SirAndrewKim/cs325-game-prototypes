Find Paradise!

  • Originality: The original game just had you remove boxes on a basic background. I added a more specific time
    and replaced the images, and added a reset mechanic.
  • Technical Merit: I still am learning JS and phaser. So this is an extremely confusing task. The resetting mechanic.
  • Prototype Postmortem: Clicking events. The hardest part was getting the reset to work. The easiest was changing the assets.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Oasis image: Background
    • Coconut image: Coconut
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: http://phaser.io/examples/v3/view/events/listen-to-game-object-event
  • High Concept: Crush the coconuts to reach the oasis!
  • Theme: Oasis and complete in 20 seconds.
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse/Clickable buttons/keyboard, a lot of randomness, Competitor/Joker, puzzle(kind of).
  • Prototype Goal: Clickable events
  • Player Experience Goals: Hand cramps and trying to reach the oasis within the allocated time.
  • Gameplay: LEFT Click on the coconut images, which reveal the background. Have to click through all of them to complete the game.
    Press SPACE to reset the game when you reach the end. (Currently bugged! Will have to return to fix).
  • Strategies: Using two fingers on the left mouse button will ensure victory!
    I would also only press space if you reach 0, otherwise, restarting the browser will reset the game better.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are stuck in a desert. But you come accross and oasis in the distance. However, a swarm of coconut block your way.
    Crush the coconuts to reach fresh water and the refreshing shade!
  • Target Audience: Anyone looking to kill 20 seconds of their time to play test!
  • Play Time: 20 seconds
