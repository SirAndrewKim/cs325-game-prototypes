  • High Concept: The entire crew has been afflicted by scurvy! Quickly fill out the board to help cure their aliment!
  • Theme: Pirates
  • Mandated Variety: Clickable Buttons, deterministic, Artist, Adventure
  • Prototype Goal: Changing and modifying the assets on a new level than before.
  • Player Experience Goals: A fun experience on filling the board.
  • Gameplay: The player must use the 6 different fruit to fill in the board.
    Blackberry = Black
    Strawberry = Red
    Lime = Green
    Lemon = Yellow
    Blueberry = Blue
    Dragonfruit = Pink
    Each fruit is clickable both on computer and mobile.
    The player has 30 clicks to fillout the entire board. If they are unable to fill the board in 30 moves, they lose.
  • Strategies: While the game does seem simple, the idea is still complex. The main strategy is to fill out the colors that boarder the main color.
    Deciding on which will yield the most color change is the goal.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The pirates have been out on sea for many days. However, they clumsyly forgot to consume fruits so they all got scurvy!
    As a nice passerby, you have to hurry to fill your bag and feed them all of the fruit you have.
  • Target Audience: Rated E: For every single person on this Earth.
  • Play Time: On average, this game takes about 1 minute. Based off play tests from family and friends.
